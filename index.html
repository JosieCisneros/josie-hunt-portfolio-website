About Me

Hello Beautiful!

My name is Josie Hunt, your local Denver REB3L Fitness instructor! Providing you a fitness platform where there is no judgment, tons of fun and a community that's got your back.

I became a REB3L instructor because I love to dance and the beauty of dancing is there is no right way to do it. In REB3L, if you're sweating and having a great time, you're doing it right.

What is REB3L

Driven by beats, sweat, and community, REB3L reveals your power, creates confidence, and builds strength through high intensity, fun full body workouts. REB3L GROOVE torches calories through HIIT style dance-based cardio. REB3L STRENGTH incorporates muscle toning movement to energizing music & choreography.

REB3L GROOVE

REB3L GROOVE uses hard hitting, easy to follow, bold choreography set to powerful & inspiring music resulting in a HIIT style empowering dance fitness format. No experience needed.

REB3L STRENGTH

REB3L STRENGTH incorporates muscle toning movement to energizing music & choreography. It creates sculpted bodies and builds physical strength using body weight, minimal equipment, and low impact/high repetition training. All fitness levels.
